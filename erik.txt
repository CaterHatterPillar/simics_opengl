* Section 2:

* Section 2.3:
* * Table 1 o 2: caption kommer för nära tabellerna, sitter ihop i vissa
    bokstäver
* * pluraltypo: s/utilize/utilizes/
* * "As such, in great numbers,": Kanske "This explains why magic instructions
    have such a great impact on performance"
* * "we conclude that": upprepning, kanske "we conclude that a magic instruction
    induces an average overhead of ..."
* * ", such as if..., ": lite klumpig mening, men jag har inget klockrent
    förslag
* * s/a third/one third/ ("en tredje" vs "en tredjedel")
* * s/increase with/increases with/
* * påståendet "native execution is far more likely" är kanske tveksamt, betänk
    simulering av en ARM-telefon. Kanske OK att bara hoppa över att motivera.

* Section 3:
* * "offers a good trade-off between ..."

    benefits of ... are" eller en helt annan formulering

    i rad som säger samma sak
* * "the performance bottleneck": s/the/one/ eller s/the/the main/?
